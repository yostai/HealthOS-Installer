## Complications

### Complication Descriptions
Examples: 
- injuries: muscle or bone, prevent user from doing certain exercises, collect restrictions.
- allergies: interfere with some diet needs, eg: getting enough protein, etc.
- diseases: can interfere with diet and exercise, collect restrictions
- addictions: carb, alcohol, medications, drugs, bulimia - some need to seek medical advice. Carbs can potentially be overcome in 3-4 days.

## Motivation
### Motivation Descriptions
Examples: 
- highly motivated, no issues, don't overdo the exercise.
- typical, will need reminding to exercise and eat right
- low motivation, will need extra push from coach.
